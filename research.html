Research Interest

I am interested in low-dimensional topology. My research to date has focused on using the tools from various Floer homology and gauge theoretic invariants to study knot concordance, homology bordism group, exotic smooth structures on 4-manifolds, knotted surfaces in 4-manifolds, and diffeomorphism groups of 4-manifolds.
